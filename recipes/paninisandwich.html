Panini Sandwich

A traditional small Italian sandwich that is pressed and grilled so that the bread has a golden, grilled crust on both sides of the sandwich while the fillings inside are compressed together.

Ingredients

  • 1 focaccia bread, quartered
  • 1/2 cup prepared basil pesto
  • 1 cup diced cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 cup shredded monterey jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a panini grill
  2. Slice each quarter of focaccia bread in half horizontally
  3. Spread each half with presto
  4. Layer bottom halves with equal amount of chicken, bell pepper, onion, and cheese
  5. Top with remaining focaccia halves, forming 4 sandwiches
  6. Grill paninis 5 minutes in the preheated grill, or until focaccia bread is golden brown and cheese is melted
