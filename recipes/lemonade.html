Thereâ€™s more to lemons than meets the eye. This fruit may pair well with some of your favorite dishes, but it also offers incredible health benefits. Lemons are packed with nutrients, promote weight loss, and have even been linked to kidney stone prevention and cancer treatment!

Ingredients

  • 1-1/3 cups sugar
  • 5 cups of water
  • cup of ice
  • 1-3/4 cups lemon juice(about 10 lemons)

Steps

  1. Juice your lemons
  2. Make syrup by combining water and sugar
  3. Pour cold water into pitcher, stir in lemon juice and pulp
  4. Add syrup
  5. Serve with ice
